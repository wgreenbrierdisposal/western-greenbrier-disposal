Learn About Us

Local Trash Collection You Can Count On

Western Greenbrier Disposal Service is a locally operated sanitation company providing dependable trash collection for homes and businesses in Western Greenbrier and surrounding areas.

We focus on simple, reliable service. That means consistent pickup schedules, clear communication, and billing options that work for busy families, landlords, and business owners. From weekly residential routes to commercial accounts and special pickups, our goal is to keep your property clean without you having to think about it.

Whether you are setting up new service, moving to a different address, or managing multiple locations, our team is here to help you choose the right service and answer any questions along the way.
