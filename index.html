Frequently Asked Questions

When should I put my cart out?

Please place your cart at the curb the night before your scheduled pickup day, with the handle facing away from the road and at least 3 feet from mailboxes, vehicles, and other obstacles.

What items cannot go in my trash?

We cannot accept household chemicals, paint, oil, fuel, batteries, tires, or large electronics in regular household trash. Contact our office if you’re unsure about a specific item.
