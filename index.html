FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I put my trash out?

Please place your bags at the curb the night before your scheduled pickup day. Make sure bags are not overloaded and are visible from the road. Any Items not bagged will not be collected.

What can’t go in my regular trash?

We cannot accept items such as paint, oil, fuel, chemicals, large electronics, batteries, or tires in regular household trash. Please contact our office if you’re unsure about specific items.

How do I start, change, or stop service?

You can request changes by calling our office or using the contact form below. Please include your name, service address, and account number (if you have one).
